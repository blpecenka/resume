Communications specialist turned web developer...

I am a multilingual Web Developer and UX Designer from Peoria, Illinois. My journey into programming began by teaching English as a Second Language (ESL) to international students via the web. My background in English and langauge studies has given me a keen eye for grammatical mistakes and concise markup. I have worked remotely as well as in group settings, which has further allowed me to expand my communicatin skills.

The web is truly a form of communication where people understand simplicity. I value a minimalist design with clean navigation for optimal effect. I have advanced communication skills for developing relationships with subject matter and clients.

Everyone just wants to communicate with each other. Whether in French, Italian, or Ruby on Rails, I strive for meaningful and clear communication through code.
